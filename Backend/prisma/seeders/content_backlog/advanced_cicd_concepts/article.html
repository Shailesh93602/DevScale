Advanced CI/CD Concepts

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich development strategies provided by modern DevOps platforms is critical for developing high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. Why use Advanced CI/CD Concepts?

Advanced CI/CD concepts—such as **GitOps**, **Infrastructure as Code (IaC)**, and **Deployment Orchestration**—allow for more powerful and efficient automation of your software project, improving its overall quality and reliability.

2. GitOps

GitOps is a DevOps methodology that uses Git repositories as the single source of truth for your infrastructure and application's desired state, automating its building, testing, and deployment from start to finish.

3. Choose a CI/CD Strategy

Choose a CI/CD strategy that works for you and your team, such as using GitOps to manage your project's infrastructure and application as intended.

Conclusion

By making advanced CI/CD concepts a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
