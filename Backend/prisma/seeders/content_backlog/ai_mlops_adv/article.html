The Discipline of MLOps: Monitoring and Drift

Training a model is only 10% of the job. MLOps (Machine Learning Operations) is the discipline of keeping that model running accurately and reliably in a changing world.

1. Data Drift: The Silent Killer

Models are trained on a snapshot of past data. But the world changes. **Data Drift** occurs when the real-world incoming data stops looking like the training data. This causes your model's accuracy to crash without any error messages. MLOps systems automatically detect this shift and trigger a re-train.

2. Model Monitoring

Professional MLOps involves tracking every prediction your model makes. You monitor for **Latency** (how long it takes), **Bias** (is it being fair?), and **Performance Decay**. This ensure that your AI remains a high-quality asset rather than a liability.

Conclusion

By mastering MLOps, you bridge the gap between "Research" and "Production." You build systems that are self-healing, transparent, and capable of maintaining industrial-grade accuracy for years.
