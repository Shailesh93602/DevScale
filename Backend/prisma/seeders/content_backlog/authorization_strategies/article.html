Understanding Authorization Strategies

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich authorization strategies—such as **RBAC** and **ABAC**—is critical for Developing high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. RBAC (Role-Based Access Control)

RBAC is an authorization strategy that allows you to correctly and securely manage exactly how each user is assigned its specific role—such as **Admin** or **User**—providing its associated permissions as intended.

2. ABAC (Attribute-Based Access Control)

ABAC is a more flexible authorization strategy that correctly uses exactly how its attributes—including the **User Location**, the **Time of Day**, and the **Sensitive Information**—are verified as intended.

Conclusion

By making authorization strategies a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
