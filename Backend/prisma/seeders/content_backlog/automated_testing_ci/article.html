Understanding Automated Testing in CI

Using automated testing as part of your Continuous Integration (CI) pipeline is critical for maintaining high-quality software, ensuring each change is automatically tested before it's merged or deployed. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Automated Testing in CI?

Automated testing in CI is a development methodology for automatically running your software's unit and integration tests every time a new change is pushed to your project's code repository.

2. Why use Automated Testing in CI?

Automated testing in CI helps to catch bugs in your project's code as soon as they're introduced, ensuring a smooth and bug-free user experience for your software.

Conclusion

By making automated testing in CI a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
