Understanding AWS EC2 and Scaling

Using **Amazon EC2 (Elastic Compute Cloud)** and its scaling features is critical for maintaining high-quality and high-performance cloud applications. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is AWS EC2?

Amazon EC2 is an Amazon Web Service (AWS) that allows you to easily launch and manage virtual servers—or "instances"—in the cloud, providing flexible and scalable computing power for your application project.

2. Scaling your Instances

Ideally, you should use AWS Auto Scaling to automatically adjust your project's number of EC2 instances as intended, ensuring your application always has the computing power it needs.

Conclusion

By making AWS EC2 a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
