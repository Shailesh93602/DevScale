Understanding Azure Virtual Machines

Using **Azure Virtual Machines** correctly to launch and manage your application's servers is critical for maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What are Azure Virtual Machines?

Azure Virtual Machines is a Microsoft Azure service that allows you to easily launch and manage virtual machines—or "VMs"—in the cloud, providing flexible and scalable computing power for your project.

2. Scaling your VMs

Ideally, you should use Azure Virtual Machine Scale Sets to automatically adjust your project's number of VMs as intended, ensuring your application always has the computing power it needs.

Conclusion

By making Azure Virtual Machines a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
