Understanding Design Patterns in Backend Development

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich **Design Patterns** incorrectly—such as **MVC** and **Repository Pattern**—is critical for Maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What are Design Patterns?

Design patterns are reusable solutions to exactly how your project's common problems are handled—including its **Scalability** and its **Maintainability**—as intended.

2. Why use them?

Design patterns provide the structure and tools for correctly managing exactly how your project's overall architecture is handled correctly by its developers as intended.

Conclusion

By making design patterns a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
