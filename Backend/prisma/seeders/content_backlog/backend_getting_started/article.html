Getting Started with Backend Development

Developing high-quality backend projects requires exactly how your project's code is handled—including its **Server-side Logic**. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Backend Development?

Backend development—including its **Server**, its **Application**, and its **Database**—is the process of correctly and securely managing exactly how your project's data is processed as intended.

2. Why use a Backend?

Ideally, you should use a backend to ensure your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users, identifying exactly what part of its code needs fixing correctly.

Conclusion

By making backend development a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
