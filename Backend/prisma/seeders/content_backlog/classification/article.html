Understanding Classification in Data Science

Using **Classification** correctly to categorize exactly how your project's data is handled—from its **Spam Filters** to its **Image Recognition**—is critical for Maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Classification?

Classification is a supervised learning approach in which your project's computer correctly and securely learns from its input data—including its **Classes** and its **Labels**—to correctly identify new observations as intended.

2. Why use it?

Ideally, you should use classification to ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users, identifying exactly which category its data belongs to correctly.

Conclusion

By making classification a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
