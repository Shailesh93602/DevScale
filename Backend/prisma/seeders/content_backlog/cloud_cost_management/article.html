Understanding Cloud Cost Management

Using cloud cost management correctly—such as with **AWS Budgets**—is critical for maintaining the sustainability of your application project. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. Why manage Cloud Costs?

Cloud cost management allows you to correctly track and manage exactly how much your project's cloud infrastructure is costing you, identifying and fixing overspending before it affects your budget.

2. Using Budgets and alerts

Ideally, you should use your cloud provider's built-in "Budgets" and "Alerts" to automatically notify you when your project's costs exceed a specific amount as intended.

Conclusion

By making cost management a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
