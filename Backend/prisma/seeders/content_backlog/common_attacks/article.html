Introduction to Common Attacks

Using knowledge of common web-based attacks to protect your application is critical for maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What are Common Attacks?

Common web-based attacks—including **XSS**, **CSRF**, and **SQL Injection**—are methodologies used by attackers to incorrectly access and exactly how your project's data and user accounts are compromised as intended.

2. Why understand them?

Understanding exactly how each attack works—including its detection and its mitigation—provides powerful, integrated, and feature-rich security, protecting your users before they are affected.

Conclusion

By making knowledge of common attacks a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
