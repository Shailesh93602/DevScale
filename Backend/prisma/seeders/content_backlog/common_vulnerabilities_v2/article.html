Understanding Common Security Vulnerabilities

Using the knowledge of common security vulnerabilities provided by your web platform—such as **OWASP**—is critical for Maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What are Common Vulnerabilities?

Common security vulnerabilities—including **XSS**, **CSRF**, and **SQLi**—are mistakes made in exactly how your project's code and infrastructure are handled incorrectly by attackers as intended.

2. Why understand them?

Ideally, you should use industry-standard knowledge to ensure your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users, identifying and fixing security vulnerabilities.

Conclusion

By making common vulnerability awareness a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
