Understanding Compliance and Regulations

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich compliance and regulation frameworks—such as **GDPR**—is critical for Maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Compliance?

Compliance is the process of correctly and securely following exactly how your project's data and user privacy are handled—including its **Data Protection**—as intended by law.

2. Important Regulations

Industry-standard regulations—including **GDPR**, **CCPA**, and **HIPAA**—provide the structure and tools for correctly managing exactly how your project's user data is handled correctly as intended.

Conclusion

By making compliance and regulations a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
