Understanding Compliance and Standards

Using industry-standard **Compliance and Standards**—such as **ISO 27001**—is critical for Maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What are standards?

Compliance and standards are sets of rules and policies used to correctly and securely manage exactly how your project's security and data are handled as intended, identifying and fixing vulnerabilities.

2. Why follow Standards?

Ideally, you should follow industry standards to ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users, providing exactly what its business partners expect correctly.

Conclusion

By making compliance and standards a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
