Understanding Container Orchestration

Using container orchestration correctly—such as via **Kubernetes**—is critical for managing high-quality, large-scale, and production-ready containerized software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Container Orchestration?

Container orchestration is a DevOps methodology for automatically managing the entire life cycle of your application's Docker containers, including their deployment, scaling, and networking.

2. Why use Orchestration?

Ideally, you should use container orchestration to correctly handle exactly how large numbers of Docker containers work together across many different servers as intended.

3. Popular Orchestration Tools

Popular container orchestration tools including **Kubernetes**, **Docker Swarm**, and **Amazon ECS** provide the structure and tools for managing complex container projects effectively.

Conclusion

By making orchestration a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
