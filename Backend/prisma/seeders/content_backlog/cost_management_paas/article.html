Understanding Cost Management in PaaS

Using cost management correctly—such as with **Heroku Usage Reports**—is critical for maintaining the sustainability of your PaaS-based project. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. Why manage PaaS Costs?

PaaS cost management allows you to correctly track and manage exactly how much your project's platform resources—such as its Dynos and its Add-ons—are costing you, identifying and fixing overspending before it affects your budget.

2. Usage-based Billing

Most PaaS platforms use usage-based billing, where you correctly pay exactly for how many platform resources your project's application uses as intended.

Conclusion

By making cost management a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
