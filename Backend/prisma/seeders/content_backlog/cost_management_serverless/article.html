Understanding Cost Management for Serverless

Using cost management correctly—such as with **AWS Cost Explorer**—is critical for maintaining the sustainability of your serverless project. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. Why manage Serverless Costs?

Serverless cost management allows you to correctly track and manage exactly how much your project's serverless code is costing you, identifying and fixing overspending before it affects your budget.

2. "Pay-per-Execution" Model

Most serverless platforms use a "Pay-per-Execution" model, where you correctly pay exactly for how many times your project's code runs and how much of its memory is used during its execution.

Conclusion

By making cost management a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
