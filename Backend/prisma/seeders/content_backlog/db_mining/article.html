Understanding Data Mining

Data mining is the process of uncovering patterns and finding anomalies and correlations within large data sets to predict outcomes.

1. Beyond Simple Queries

While standard database queries tell you 'what happened', data mining uses statistical models and machine learning algorithms to tell you 'why it happened' and 'what might happen next'.

2. Key Techniques

  • Classification: Assigning data points to predefined groups.
  • Clustering: Discovering groups with similar characteristics within the data.
  • Association Rule Learning: Finding interesting relationships between variables.
  • Anomaly Detection: Identifying data points that deviate from the normal pattern.

Conclusion

Data mining is a bridge between databases and machine learning, allowing organizations to turn their massive datasets into a competitive advantage through predictive analysis.
