Advanced Docker in CI/CD

Using advanced Docker strategies correctly within your CI/CD pipeline is critical for maintaining high-quality, efficient, and production-ready software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. Why use Advanced Docker in CI/CD?

Advanced Docker strategies—such as **Multi-Stage Builds**, **Layer Caching**, and **Image Versioning**—allow for more powerful and efficient automation of your software project's building and deployment.

2. Multi-Stage Builds

Ideally, you should use Docker's multi-stage builds to correctly handle exactly how your application project's production-ready container image is built as intended, reducing its final image size.

Conclusion

By making Docker a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
