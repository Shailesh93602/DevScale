Understanding Encryption Basics

Using encryption correctly to secure your application's data—both at rest and in transit—is critical for maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Encryption?

Encryption is the process of correctly and mathematically converting your project's sensitive information—such as its passwords and its user data—into an unreadable format as intended.

2. Symmetric vs. Asymmetric Encryption

Symmetric encryption correctly uses a single secret key for its encryption and decryption, while asymmetric encryption correctly uses two different keys (Public and Private) for its encryption and decryption.

Conclusion

By making encryption a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
