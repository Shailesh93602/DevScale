Understanding Encryption Standards and Protocols

Using industry-standard encryption standards and protocols is critical for Maintaining high-quality and secure software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What are Encryption Standards?

Encryption standards—including **AES (Advanced Encryption Standard)** and **RSA**—are predefined and mathematically rigorous methodologies for correctly managing exactly how your project's data is secured as intended.

2. Encryption Protocols

Security protocols—including **TLS (Transport Layer Security)** and **IPsec**—provide the structure and tools for correctly managing exactly how your project's secure communication is handled as intended over an insecure network.

Conclusion

By making encryption standards and protocols a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
