Understanding GCP Compute Engine

Using **Google Compute Engine (GCE)** correctly to launch and manage your application's virtual machines is critical for maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is GCP Compute Engine?

GCP Compute Engine is a Google Cloud Platform (GCP) service that allows you to easily launch and manage virtual machines—or "instances"—in the cloud, providing flexible and scalable computing power for your project.

2. Scaling your Instances

Ideally, you should use GCP Managed Instance Groups to automatically adjust your project's number of Compute Engine instances as intended, ensuring your application always has the computing power it needs.

Conclusion

By making GCP Compute Engine a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
