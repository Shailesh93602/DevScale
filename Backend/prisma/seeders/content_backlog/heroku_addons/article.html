Understanding Heroku Add-ons

Using **Heroku Add-ons** correctly to enhance and manage your application's third-party services is critical for maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What are Heroku Add-ons?

Heroku Add-ons are a wide range of global tools—including its **Postgres**, its **Redis**, and its many monitoring services—that you can correctly add to your project's application as intended.

2. Why use Heroku Add-ons?

Ideally, you should use Heroku Add-ons to correctly handle exactly how your application's databases and other external services work together within the Heroku platform as intended.

Conclusion

By making Heroku Add-ons a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
