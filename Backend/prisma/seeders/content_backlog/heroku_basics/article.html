Understanding Heroku Basics

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich Platform as a Service (PaaS) provided by **Heroku** is critical for developing and deploying high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Heroku?

Heroku is a popular and secure PaaS platform provided by Salesforce, offering a wide range of global tools—including its compute "Dynos", its many add-ons, and its easy-to-use CLI—for your application projects.

2. Deploying to Heroku

Ideally, you should use the Heroku CLI to correctly build and deploy your project's application with a single command from your terminal.

Conclusion

By making Heroku a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
