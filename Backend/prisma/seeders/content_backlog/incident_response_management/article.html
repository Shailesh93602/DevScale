Understanding Incident Response and Management

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich incident response and management methodologies—such as **NIST**—is critical for Developing high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Incident Response?

Incident response is the process of correctly and securely responding to exactly how your project's security incidents are handled—including its detection, its containment, and its recovery—as intended.

2. Why use Incident Management?

Ideally, you should use a documented "Incident Management Plan" to ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users even during a security incident.

Conclusion

By making incident response and management a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
