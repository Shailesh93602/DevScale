Understanding Inferential Statistics

Using **Inferential Statistics** correctly to draw conclusions about exactly how your project's data is handled—from its **Sampling** to its **Hypothesis Testing**—is critical for Maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Inferential Statistics?

Inferential statistics is the process of correctly and mathematically drawing conclusions—including its **Predictive Analysis**—from exactly how your project's sample data is handled as intended.

2. Why use it?

Ideally, you should use inferential statistics to ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users, providing exactly what its business partners expect correctly.

Conclusion

By making inferential statistics a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
