Understanding Input Validation & Sanitization

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich tools for **Input Validation** and **Sanitization** is critical for Maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. Input Validation

Input validation is the process of correctly and securely verifying exactly what data is being provided by your project's users—including its **Format**, its **Type**, and its **Length**—as intended.

2. Input Sanitization

Input sanitization correctly cleans and modifies your application's user-provided data—including its **HTML Characters** and its **Scripts**—to ensure it is safe to use in its execution as intended.

Conclusion

By making input validation and sanitization a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
