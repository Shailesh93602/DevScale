Integration with CI/CD Pipelines

Using automated unit testing as part of your CI/CD pipeline is critical for maintaining high-quality software, ensuring each change is automatically tested before it's merged or deployed. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is CI/CD?

CI/CD is a software development methodology that automates the building, testing, and deployment of your code changes, ensuring that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and organized for your users.

2. Automating Tests in your CI/CD pipeline

By integrating your unit tests directly into your CI/CD pipeline (e.g., via GitHub Actions or GitLab), you can ensure that your tests are automatically run on every new commit and pull request.

3. Choosing an Automation Strategy

Choose an automation strategy that works for you and your team, such as automated building and testing on every pull request, or periodically on every commit into the main branch.

Conclusion

By making unit testing a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
