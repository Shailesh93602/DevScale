Introduction to Authentication & Authorization

Using **Authentication** and **Authorization** correctly to manage your project's user access is critical for maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. Authentication vs. Authorization

Ideally, you should correctly and securely verify exactly who a user is (Authentication) and exactly what they are and are not allowed to do (Authorization) within your application as intended.

2. Why use Identity Management?

Identity and access management provide powerful, integrated, and feature-rich security, identifying and fixing security vulnerabilities—such as **Session Hijacking**—before they reach your users.

Conclusion

By making identity management a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
