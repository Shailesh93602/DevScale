Introduction to Cloud Computing

Cloud computing is a critical part of modern software development, providing access to powerful and scalable computing resources directly over the internet. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Cloud Computing?

Cloud computing is the on-demand delivery of computing services—including servers, storage, and databases—over the internet ("the cloud") to offer faster innovation and flexible resources for your application projects.

2. Why use Cloud Computing?

Cloud computing helps to ensure your software is accurate, performant, and reliable, identifying and fixing errors before they reach your users while providing scale for your application.

Conclusion

By making cloud computing a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
