Introduction to Encryption

Using encryption correctly to protect your application's data is critical for maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Encryption?

Encryption is the process of correctly and mathematically converting your project's sensitive data—such as its passwords and its tokens—into an unreadable format as intended.

2. Why use Encryption?

Encryption helps to ensure your software is accurate, performant, and reliable, protecting your application's most valuable asset—its data—from unauthorized access as intended.

Conclusion

By making encryption a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
