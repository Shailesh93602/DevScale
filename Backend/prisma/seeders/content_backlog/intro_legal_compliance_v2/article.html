Introduction to Legal & Compliance

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich legal and compliance frameworks is critical for Maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Legal Compliance?

Legal compliance is the process of correctly and securely following exactly how your project's data and user privacy are handled—including its **Regulations** and its **Standards**—as intended by law.

2. Why use Compliance?

Compliance helps ensure your software is accurate, performant, and reliable, identifying and fixing security vulnerabilities by providing its legal and ethical structure correctly as intended.

Conclusion

By making legal and compliance awareness a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
