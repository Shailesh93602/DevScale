Introduction to Platform as a Service (PaaS)

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich development platform provided by **Platform as a Service (PaaS)** is critical for developing high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is PaaS?

Platform as a Service (PaaS) is a cloud computing methodology where you can correctly build and deploy your project's application without manage any underlying infrastructure—such as its server machines—directly.

2. Why use PaaS?

PaaS helps to ensure your software is accurate, performant, and reliable, identifying and fixing errors before they reach your users, while providing the structure and tools for your application project.

Conclusion

By making PaaS a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
