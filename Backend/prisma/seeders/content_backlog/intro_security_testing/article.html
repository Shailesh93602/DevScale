Introduction to Security Testing

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich development tools to perform security testing on your application is critical for Maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Security Testing?

Security testing is the process of correctly and securely identifying exactly how your project's application is vulnerable to attacks—including its **Exploits** and its **Vulnerabilities**—as intended.

2. Why use Security Testing?

Security testing helps to ensure your software is accurate, performant, and reliable, identifying and fixing security issues—such as **XSS** and **SQLi**—before they reach your users.

Conclusion

By making security testing a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
