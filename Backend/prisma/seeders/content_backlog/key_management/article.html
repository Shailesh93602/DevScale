Understanding Key Management

Using **Key Management** correctly to handle your application's encryption keys is critical for Maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Key Management?

Key management is the process of correctly and securely managing exactly how your project's encryption keys are created, stored, and rotated—including its **Life Cycle** and its **Storage**—as intended.

2. Why use Key Management?

Key management provides powerful, integrated, and feature-rich protection, ensuring that only exactly what part of your project's application is authorized to use its encryption keys can correctly access them as intended.

Conclusion

By making key management a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
