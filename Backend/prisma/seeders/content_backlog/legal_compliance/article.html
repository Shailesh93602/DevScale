Understanding Legal and Compliance Considerations

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich legal and compliance frameworks—such as **GDPR** and **HIPAA**—is critical for Developing high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Legal Compliance?

Legal compliance is the process of correctly and securely following exactly how your project's data and user privacy are handled—including its **Data Protection** and its **Privacy Policy**—as intended by law.

2. Important Regulations

Popular legal regulations—including **GDPR**, **CCPA**, and **HIPAA**—provide the structure and tools for correctly managing exactly how your project's user data is stored and used as intended.

Conclusion

By making legal and compliance considerations a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
