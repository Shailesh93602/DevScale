Understanding Logging and Monitoring for Security

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich logging and monitoring tools provided by your backend platform—such as **Winston** and **Morgan**—is critical for Maintaining high-quality and secure software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. Why log for Security?

Logging for security correctly allows you to correctly track and manage exactly how your project's application is behaving—identifying and fixing security incidents—such as **Failed Login Attempts**—before they reach your users.

2. Monitoring your Logs

Ideally, you should monitor your project's security logs for any unusual activity correctly as intended—ensuring you use its historical data for your auditing and incident response goals.

Conclusion

By making logging and monitoring a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
