Understanding Manual Security Testing

Using **Manual Security Testing** correctly to identify exactly how your project's application is vulnerable to security risks—such as through its **Business Logic**—is critical for Maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Manual Testing?

Manual security testing is the process of correctly and securely reviewing exactly how your project's source code and application are handled—including its **Code Reviews** and its **Session Management**—as intended.

2. Why use Manual Testing?

Ideally, you should use manual testing to ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users, identifying and fixing security vulnerabilities before it reaches them.

Conclusion

By making manual security testing a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
