Machine Learning Design Patterns

As ML systems move into production, design patterns help address common challenges in data representation, model training, and serving. These patterns ensure your ML system is robust, scalable, and maintainable.

1. Feature Engineering Patterns

Patterns like Feature Crosses (combining features) and Embeddings (representing categorical data as vectors) are essential for capturing complex relationships in your data.

2. Training Patterns

Common training patterns include Transfer Learning (using a pre-trained model as a starting point) and Hyperparameter Tuning patterns to systematically find the best model configuration.

3. Serving Patterns

When deploying, patterns like Model Versioning, Canary Deployment, and A/B Testing ensure that you can safely roll out new models and monitor their real-world performance.

Conclusion

By applying established ML design patterns, you can avoid common pitfalls and build production-ready AI systems that provide consistent and reliable value.
