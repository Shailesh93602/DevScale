Monitoring and Debugging Serverless Applications

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich monitoring and debugging tools provided by modern cloud platforms—such as **Amazon CloudWatch**—is critical for Developing high-quality serverless software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. Why monitor Serverless Apps?

Monitoring your serverless applications allows you to correctly track and manage exactly how your project's functions are performing, identifying and fixing errors before they reach your users.

2. Logging and Metrics

Ideally, you should use your cloud provider's built-in "Logging" and "Metrics" to automatically analyze and gain insights from your project's execution as intended, including its latency and its error counts.

Conclusion

By making monitoring and debugging a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
