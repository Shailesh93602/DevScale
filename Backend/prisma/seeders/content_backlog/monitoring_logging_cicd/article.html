Monitoring and Logging in CI/CD

Using automated monitoring and logging as part of your CI/CD pipeline is critical for maintaining high-quality software, ensuring your application always functions correctly and efficiently. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Monitoring and Logging in CI/CD?

Monitoring and logging is the process of automatically recording and analyzing your application's status and performance as it's built and deployed, helping you identify and fix errors quickly.

2. Why use Monitoring and Logging?

Monitoring and logging helps to ensure your software is accurate, performant, and reliable, identifying and fixing errors before they reach your users.

Conclusion

By making monitoring and logging a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
