Understanding OWASP ZAP

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich **OWASP ZAP** (Zed Attack Proxy) is critical for Maintaining high-quality and secure software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is OWASP ZAP?

OWASP ZAP is an open-source security tool that allows you to correctly and securely perform exactly how its DAST (Dynamic Application Security Testing) is conducted—including its **Spidering** and its **Active Scanning**—as intended.

2. Why use ZAP?

OWASP ZAP helps to ensure your software is accurate, performant, and reliable, identifying and fixing security vulnerabilities—such as **Insecure Configurations**—before they reach your users.

Conclusion

By making OWASP ZAP a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
