Understanding PaaS Troubleshooting and Support

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich troubleshooting and support tools provided by your PaaS platform—such as its **CLI Log Streaming**—is critical for Developing high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. Why troubleshoot on PaaS?

Troubleshooting correctly on a PaaS platform allows you to correctly track and manage exactly how your project's application is performing, and to identify and fix errors before they reach your users.

2. Log Streaming

Ideally, you should use your PaaS platform's built-in "Log Streaming" to automatically view exactly how your project's application is behaving in its production-ready environment as intended.

Conclusion

By making troubleshooting and support a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
