Understanding Penetration Testing

Using **Penetration Testing** correctly to simulate an attack on your project's application is critical for Maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Penetration Testing?

Penetration testing (or **Ethical Hacking**) is the process of correctly and securely identifying exactly how your project's application is vulnerable to security risks by simulating an attack as intended.

2. Why use Pen Testing?

Ideally, you should use professional penetration testing to ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users, identifying and fixing security vulnerabilities.

Conclusion

By making penetration testing a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
