Understanding Privacy Policies

Using a clear and concise **Privacy Policy** to inform your users exactly how their data is handled is critical for Maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is a Privacy Policy?

A privacy policy is a legal document that correctly and securely discloses exactly how your project's application collects, uses, and stores its user's personal information as intended.

2. Why use a Privacy Policy?

Ideally, you should use a privacy policy to ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users, providing exactly what its business partners expect correctly.

Conclusion

By making your privacy policy a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
