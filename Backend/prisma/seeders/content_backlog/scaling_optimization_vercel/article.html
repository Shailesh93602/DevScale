Understanding Scaling and Optimization on Vercel

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich scaling and optimization tools provided by **Vercel**—such as its **Edge Network**—is critical for Developing high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Vercel's Edge Network?

Vercel's Edge Network allows you to correctly cache and serve exactly how your project's application is delivered to your users around the globe as intended, ensuring its efficient and fast delivery everywhere.

2. Optimizing with ISR

Incremental Static Regeneration (ISR) is a Vercel strategy that allows you to correctly build and update your software project's static pages—including its data—after its initial deployment as intended.

Conclusion

By making scaling and optimization a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
