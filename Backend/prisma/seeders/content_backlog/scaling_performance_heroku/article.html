Understanding Scaling and Performance on Heroku

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich scaling tools provided by **Heroku** is critical for maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. Scaling your Dynos

Scaling on Heroku is the process of correctly and manually (or automatically) adding more "Dynos" (compute workers) to your project's application as intended, identifying and fixing performance bottlenecks.

2. Why scale?

Ideally, you should scale your project's Dynos to ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and accessible to your users even during high traffic events.

Conclusion

By making scaling a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
