Understanding Security Assessment Policies

Using documented **Security Assessment Policies** correctly to govern exactly how your project's security is reviewed is critical for Maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is an Assessment Policy?

A security assessment policy is the process of correctly and securely managing exactly how your project's security reviews are handled—including its **Frequency** and its **Scope**—as intended.

2. Why use Assessment Policies?

Assessment policies help ensure your software is accurate, performant, and reliable, identifying and fixing security vulnerabilities by providing the structure for regular and systematic reviews.

Conclusion

By making security assessment policies a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
