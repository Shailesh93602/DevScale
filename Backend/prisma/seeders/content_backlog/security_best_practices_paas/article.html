Understanding Security Best Practices on PaaS

Using security best practices correctly—such as for **Environment Variable Management**—is critical for maintaining high-quality software on your PaaS platform. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. Why use Security Best Practices on PaaS?

PaaS security best practices allow you to correctly secure your project's application and its data, identifying and fixing security vulnerabilities before they reach your users.

2. Secure your Environment Variables

Ideally, you should store and manage your software project's secret keys and other environment variables within your PaaS platform's dedicated secure storage as intended.

Conclusion

By making security best practices a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
