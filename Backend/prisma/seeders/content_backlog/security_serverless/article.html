Understanding Security in Serverless Architecture

Using serverless security correctly is critical for maintaining high-quality software, ensuring your application and its data are correctly secured. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Serverless Security?

Serverless security is the process of correctly securing your project's serverless functions and their data, identifying and fixing security vulnerabilities before they reach your users.

2. Principle of Least Privilege

The principle of least privilege identifies exactly what parts of your project's serverless application should have access to what parts of your data and infrastructure as intended, ensuring they only have the minimum amount of access necessary.

Conclusion

By making serverless security a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
