Understanding Session Management

Using secure **Session Management** correctly to handle your project's user state is critical for maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Session Management?

Session management is the process of correctly and securely managing exactly how your project's authenticated user's state is stored and retrieved—including its **Cookies** and its **Session IDs**—as intended.

2. Session Security

Ideally, you should use **HttpOnly** and **Secure** flags for your application's cookies to ensure that your user's session data remains safe and protected from unauthorized access correctly as intended.

Conclusion

By making session management a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
