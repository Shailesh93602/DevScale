Understanding Types of Encryption

Using the different **Types of Encryption** correctly to secure your application's data is critical for Maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. Symmetric Encryption

Symmetric encryption correctly uses a single secret key for its encryption and decryption, allowing for powerful and efficient protection of exactly how your project's data is handled as intended.

2. Asymmetric Encryption

Asymmetric encryption correctly uses two different keys (Public and Private) for its encryption and decryption—identifying and fixing security vulnerabilities by allowing for its secure communication over an insecure network.

Conclusion

By making symmetric and asymmetric encryption a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
