Understanding User Management and Roles

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich user management and role-based access control (RBAC) tools is critical for Maintaining high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is User Management?

User management is the process of correctly and securely managing exactly how your project's users are created, updated, and deleted—including its **User Profiles** and its **Account Settings**—as intended.

2. Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

RBAC allows you to correctly assign exactly the right level of access to each user based on their specific role within your application correctly, identifying and fixing security vulnerabilities before they reach your users.

Conclusion

By making user management and roles a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
