Understanding Vercel Basics

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich Platform as a Service (PaaS) provided by **Vercel** is critical for developing and deploying high-quality frontend software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What is Vercel?

Vercel is a popular and secure PaaS platform specializing in frontend frameworks—including **Next.js**, **React**, and **Vue**—offering a wide range of global tools for your project's deployment as intended.

2. Deploying to Vercel

Ideally, you should connect your project's repository—such as on **GitHub**—to Vercel to automatically build and deploy your application every time you make a commit.

Conclusion

By making Vercel a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its data—remains safe and performant for your users.
