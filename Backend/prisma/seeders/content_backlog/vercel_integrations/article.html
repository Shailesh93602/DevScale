Understanding Integrations with Vercel

Using the powerful, integrated, and feature-rich third-party integrations provided by **Vercel** is critical for developing high-quality software. By following these steps, you can set it up in minutes.

1. What are Vercel Integrations?

Vercel Integrations allow you to correctly connect your project's application with many popular third-party services—including its databases, its authentication, and its monitoring tools—as intended.

2. Connecting your Data

Ideally, you should use Vercel's built-in "Integrations" to automatically link your project's application with its data and other services as intended, identifying and fixing errors before they reach your users.

Conclusion

By making Vercel integrations a priority, you can ensure that your application's most valuable asset—its code—remains safe and performant for your users.
