Choosing a Table Format: Iceberg vs Delta Lake vs Hudi

One of the most consequential architectural decisions a Data Engineer at a new company faces is: "Which open table format should we standardize on?" Apache Iceberg, Delta Lake, and Apache Hudi are all capable mature technologies. The best choice depends on your team's ecosystem, use case, and organizational constraints rather than any single definitive technical superiority.

When to Choose Apache Iceberg

Choose Iceberg if:

  • You need true multi-engine support: Your tables must be queryable by Spark, Flink, Trino, Presto, Snowflake, and BigQuery simultaneously without vendor lock-in.
  • You are partnering with major cloud providers: AWS Glue, AWS EMR, and Google BigQuery all have first-class Iceberg integration.
  • You want the strongest Schema Evolution guarantees, including column renaming and type promotion without rewriting data.

When to Choose Delta Lake

Choose Delta Lake if:

  • Your data platform is built on Databricks or Databricks-managed Spark clusters. Delta Lake's optimizations (Z-Ordering, Auto-Optimize, Auto-Compaction) are deeply integrated into the Databricks Runtime.
  • You need first-class Python and Spark DataFrame API integration with no friction.
  • Your team already uses Delta-native features like Change Data Feed and Liquid Clustering.

When to Choose Apache Hudi

Choose Hudi if:

  • Your primary use case is high-frequency, near-real-time CDC ingestion from relational databases at very high update rates.
  • You need Merge-on-Read tables to achieve sub-minute data freshness in your Data Lake.
  • Your team is already established on the AWS ecosystem (Amazon EMR has excellent Hudi support via Apache Hudi's AWS EMR integration profile).

Industry Trend (2024-2025)

As of 2025, Apache Iceberg is the most rapidly growing format and is increasingly becoming the de facto open standard, especially as major cloud vendors (AWS Glue, Google BigQuery, Snowflake, Azure Data Lake) all add first-class native Iceberg support. Unless you are a committed Databricks shop, new greenfield data lake projects are increasingly choosing Iceberg by default.
